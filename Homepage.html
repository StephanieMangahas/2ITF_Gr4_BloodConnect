TARLAC MEDICAL CENTER

About US

The Tarlac Medical Society is made up of Tarlac-based medical practitioners who share the common goal of achieving the highest levels of medical knowledge and skills through ongoing education and research. Members of the Association are physicians who have been duly licensed to practice medicine in the Philippines and whose names have been entered into the Association's membership record. The Organization was established as a non-profit, non-stock professional organization dedicated to bringing together and uniting the entire Philippine medical profession while also advancing medical science, extending medical knowledge, and promoting medical practice.


Mission

The Tarlac Medical Society aims to extend medical knowledge and advance medical science within the Philippines through the unification of the entire medical profession, in order to promote the standards of overall medical education and practice; as well as to serve as an authoritative source of knowledge and information regarding these fields. The organization also aims to ensure the enactment of just medical laws by promoting and improving knowledge, information, and the practice of the medical profession within the Philippines. In addition, connections and fraternal relations among physicians and allied professionals are encouraged to aid in further achieving higher medical knowledge, and to protect the rights and prerogatives of physicians on their roles in the preservation of life and health within the society.

FOR BLOOD DONORS:

If you are willing to donate blood and make a difference in someone's life,
BloodConnect provides you with a convenient and user-friendly platform to
connect with blood donation centers and hospitals. Here's how it works:

1. Create a Donor Profile: bStart by creating your donor profile on our website.
Provide necessary information such as your contact details, blood type, and
any specific preferences or restrictions you may have.

2. Connect with Centers and Hospitals: Once you find a compatible donation
opportunity, you can directly contact the blood centers and hospitals to
inquire about their current blood donation requirements and availability.

2. Connect with Centers and Hospitals: Our platform allows you to search
for blood centers, hospitals, and blood drives in your area that require your
specific blood type. Filter the results based on location, date, and time to find
the most suitable donation opportunities.

3. Donate with Ease: Visit the designated blood center or hospital based on
the information provided by them. The healthcare professionals will guide you
through the donation process, ensuring your comfort and safety. Afterward,
take a moment to replenish with refreshments and rest as needed.



4. Spread the Word: Encourage friends, family, and people you know to donate.
Introduce BloodConnect, inform them about the process, and save lives!
